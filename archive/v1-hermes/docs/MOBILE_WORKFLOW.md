# Hermes Mobile Workflow

1. Wake up and open ChatGPT.
2. Navigate to your defined daily briefing project/chat (scheduled to run at 05:30).
3. Copy the **Daily Briefing JSON** output.
4. Navigate to your defined **Intelligence Amplifier** chat (configured with the INTELLIGENCE_AMPLIFIER_PROMPT.md prompt).
5. Paste the Daily Briefing JSON and hit enter.
6. While it generates, open the Hermes app.
7. The app detects no briefing for today and shows the **Step 1: Paste Daily Briefing** screen. Paste your clipboard (validating automatically).
8. The app then advances to **Step 2: Paste Intelligence Amplifier**.
9. Switch back to ChatGPT, copy the finished Amplifier JSON output.
10. Switch back to Hermes, paste the Amplifier JSON.
11. After a seamless loading animation, your fully amplified, top-tier daily briefing is ready to read.
